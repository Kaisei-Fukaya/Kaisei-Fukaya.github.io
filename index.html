Yonder is a tranquil game about delivering packages to your neighbours and helping the community.

I was inspired by "Zelda: Wind Waker" and "Animal Crossing"; thinking that I would personally enjoy a game with the simple and relaxed gameplay loop of "Animal Crossing" and travelling by boat. I decided to use a Toon style shader, which ended up making the game look reminiscent of "Untitled Goose Game". I really enjoyed crafting the world and I plan on eventually expanding upon this idea.
